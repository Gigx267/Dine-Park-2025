About Us

We are a transformative initiative designed to empower young adults and teenagers. Through mentorship, training, and capacity building, we aim to equip them with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed and contribute positively to their communities. Project 100 is a CSR initiative by Dine & Pack, driven by a vision of creating sustainable change. By providing structured training programs, mentorship, and support in areas like business startup, bookkeeping, and more, we are working to address critical needs in our society and empower the less privileged.Project 100 is committed to making a lasting impact.Participants will be presented certificates to recognize their completion of the program. Special awards for outstanding performance in areas like leadership, creativity, and resilience can also be given. This adds a personal touch and motivates participants to continue their journey of growth, Each participant will have the opportunity to present their business idea, showcasing what they have learned. This presentation will allow them to demonstrate their understanding of the skills taught, including business management, marketing, and financial planning. It will also provide valuable feedback from mentors and peers

Goals and Objectives

Project 100 is a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative designed to empower young adults and teenagers in Nigeria, particularly those from underprivileged backgrounds, through comprehensive training, mentorship, and capacity-building programs. The project aims to equip participants with the skills, knowledge, and resources necessary to break the cycle of poverty and become active, contributing members of society.

Our Approach

We adopt a hands-on, collaborative approach, working closely with participants to tailor our programs to their needs and aspirations. Our team of experienced mentors and trainers is dedicated to providing high-quality support and fostering a nurturing environment for growth. By partnering with Project 100, you have the opportunity to contribute to a transformative initiative that makes a tangible difference in the lives of young individuals and the broader community. Together, we can drive meaningful change and create a brighter future for all.

How To Support

Donate to Project 100 to support our training programs, mentorship initiatives, and educational resources for underprivileged Nigerian youth. Volunteer your time to provide guidance and expertise as a mentor or trainer. Spread the word about our mission to empower young people and create a more equitable society. Partner with Project 100 on joint initiatives to expand our reach and impact communities across Nigeria.

Who we are

We are a community-driven initiative dedicated to providing opportunities for underprivileged teenagers and young adults. Recognizing the challenges they face in today's economic climate, we aim to empower them to chart a new course in life. By providing these resources, we aim to foster entrepreneurship, promote education, and empower young people to become self-sufficient.
